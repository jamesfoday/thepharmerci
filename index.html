About US

Pharmerci contributes to improve healthcare across Africa by sourcing, in-licensing, manufacturing, representing, and distributing high-quality products and information across the continent. Pharmerci distributes products on the continent through its sister company Nouvel.

How we work

DISTRIBUTION

Looking for a trusted partner or wondering if your product is a fit for Africa? Our expertise is sourcing the best products for the markets. Private label and network building. Our in-depth knowledge of the markets and its network of local importers and distributors ensure your brand is the best solution for the right demand. We evaluate local needs, local supply chains, regulations and policies, changing expectations, values and constraints in the market to offer the best supply chain possibility for each product.

IN-LICENCING AND REPRESENTATION

Despite seemingly many similarities across African countries, the various nations have distinctly different local market conditions and dynamics. We take pride in our great relationship with some national authorities, healthcare professionals, wholesalers, diverse private organizations in the healthcare space and pharmacy chains. Our strength is the business experience, category competence and local expertise to succeed in each country.
