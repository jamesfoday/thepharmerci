Biography

Dr. Gerard Mengang

Co-Founder & COO, Nouvel sister company

Dr. Gerard Mengang Co-Founder & Chief Operating Officer, Innovarx Global Health | Founder, Nouvel Health care System Dr. Gerard Mengang is a highly accomplished healthcare leader with expertise in pharmacy, engineering, and healthcare systems management. He holds a Doctorate of Pharmacy and a PhD in Outcomes Research, along with degrees in engineering sciences. Dr. Mengang co-founded Innovarx Global Health, where he leads the development of innovative healthcare solutions. As the founder of Nouvel Health System, a comprehensive healthcare system with an integrated Electronic Medical Records (EMR) platform, Dr. Mengang is committed to improving access to high-quality care. Nouvel Health System offers a range of services, including telemedicine, diagnostic testing, and specialty care, with a focus on enhancing patient outcomes through advanced technologies. With over 15 years of experience, Dr. Mengang is dedicated to transforming healthcare delivery and accessibility.
