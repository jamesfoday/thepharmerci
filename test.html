Biography

Ateh Atabong

Pharmacist | Innovation Consultant | Entrepreneur

Ateh Atabong: Pharmacist | Innovation Consultant | Entrepreneur With an 18-year career in the pharmaceutical industry, Ateh Atabong has held key leadership roles across Europe, spanning Product Development, Manufacturing, Quality Assurance, and Supply Chain Management. Over the past five years, he has been dedicated to strengthening pharmaceutical supply chains across Europe and Asia, ensuring efficiency, reliability, and innovation in a critical global industry. Beyond his pharmaceutical expertise, Ateh is an accomplished Innovation Consultant and Entrepreneur, specializing in helping businesses unlock greater value through innovation and Design Thinking. Leveraging strategic insights from marketing and design, he collaborates with companies to strengthen customer relationships, future-proof their strategies, and drive innovation in today’s fast-evolving marketplace. Ateh holds a Pharmacy degree (MPharm) alongside multiple certifications in Business Management, Innovation, Design Thinking, and Marketing. He is a trusted advisor for Nordic companies expanding into the African market, providing expert market insights, brand strategy, and growth guidance. Additionally, Ateh serves on the board of seven companies in Norway and Kenya and is an angel investor in five, supporting visionary businesses on their path to success.
