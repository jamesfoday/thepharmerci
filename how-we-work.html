How we Work

DISTRIBUTION

Looking for a trusted partner or wondering if your product is a fit for Africa?

Our expertise is sourcing the best products for the markets. Private label and network building. Our in-depth knowledge of the markets and its network of local importers and distributors ensure your brand is the best solution for the right demand. We evaluate local needs, local supply chains, regulations and policies, changing expectations, values and constraints in the market to offer the best supply chain possibility for each product. If you are looking for a long-term distribution agreement or licensing partner for your products you will find that Pharmerci can offer a streamlined process and an effective supply chain organization with excellent communication skills and efficient decision processes. We are flexible and have experience with different partnership arrangements – from plain distribution agreements to licensing deals and more complex structures with joint ownership.
