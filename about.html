About us

Overview

Pharmerci contributes to improve healthcare across Africa by sourcing, in-licensing, manufacturing, representing, and distributing high-quality products and information across the continent. Pharmerci distributes products on the continent through its sister company Nouvel.

In many parts of the developing world, particularly in Africa, the lack of access to basic healthcare knowledge leads to preventable illnesses, unnecessary financial burdens, and an overreliance on outdated medical practices. Worse yet, counterfeit drugs and unregulated supply chains further complicate efforts to provide care where it’s needed most.

That’s where Pharmerci comes in

We are committed to empowering individuals with the knowledge they need to make informed healthcare decisions. By leveraging the reach of the internet, we are able to deliver high-quality, locally relevant healthcare education across the continent—from bustling urban centers to the most remote rural areas.

Our mission is simple

To provide access to reliable, affordable healthcare products, information, and IT-related services that improve lives—supporting health and well-being for all, at every stage of life.
